Welcome Students!

Start your new life as a student in the UK, but not before learning some tips and tricks

Make sure you find the right place to stay! You can look for a spare room, or look for your ideal flatmate.


We suggest to immediately register with a GP (depending on your post code you can register with one close to uni or your local area) to avoid waiting times during the winter months when everyone gets sick.


Make the most of your money by signing up for a student bank account, and using sites like UniDays for discounts.


Join a sports club no matter whether you’re a newbie or you want to take part in higher-level competitive sports. There is something for everyone. The easiest way to make new friends is in a team sport, especially on campus.


Getting a student oyster and railcard helps to save money, especially when you decide to visit cities and sights all over the country or travel across London regularly. Stay safe and use Safe & the City app in London.


There are so many great places in the UK, lots of day trips or weekend adventures are organised by travel agencies especially for students. If you want to explore the country on your own, trains and busses run across the country.


Enjoy your stay!
